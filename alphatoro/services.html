SERVICES

How ALPHATORO helps.

Six disciplines, applied in order. Pricing is the lead because pricing is where most commercial firms lose before they ever deliver a technical volume.

01

Pricing Strategy and Cost Volume Development

Our expert pricers build winning cost volumes with deep fluency in Service Contract Act wage determinations, Collective Bargaining Agreement flow-down, DCAA-compliant indirect rate structures, and the nuanced mechanics that separate federal audit pricing from commercial engagement pricing. Pricing is where most commercial firms lose before they ever deliver a technical volume. We win it.

  • SCA wage determination
  • CBA flow-down
  • DCAA-compliant indirects
  • Price-to-win analysis
  • Cost realism
  • Fringe and burden modeling
02

Opportunity Pipeline

We surface qualified federal opportunities screened for winability, not volume. Every opportunity is filtered against incumbent dynamics, set-aside posture, agency buying patterns, and our client's real competitive position. Clients get a short, high-signal list they can actually execute against.

  • SAM.gov monitoring
  • Forecast tracking
  • Agency buying patterns
  • Set-aside filtering
03

Incumbent Intelligence

We map current holders, CPARS records, pricing posture, and real weaknesses. When we walk into a capture, we already know who we are taking work from and where they are exposed. That intelligence shapes the technical approach, the price, and the teaming call.

  • CPARS analysis
  • Incumbent cost mapping
  • Customer sentiment
  • Performance gap identification
04

Past Performance Translation

We rewrite commercial delivery into federal Section M language. Strong commercial performance rarely sells itself to a federal evaluator. We translate scope, outcomes, and metrics into the exact form evaluators are trained to score against.

  • Section L and M alignment
  • PWS-to-commercial mapping
  • Quantified outcomes
  • Relevance narratives
05

Teaming Architecture

We broker teaming agreements with complementary primes, set-aside partners, and specialty subs. The right team sheet is often the difference between a shortlist and a shutout. We build it with intention, not convenience.

  • Prime / sub pairing
  • 8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB
  • TA and NDA drafting
  • Workshare modeling
06

Proposal Writing

Full proposal writing support on selected opportunities. Technical volumes, management approach, staffing plans, transition plans, and orals. Clients stay close to the delivery narrative. We own the structure, compliance, and quality.

  • Technical and management volumes
  • Compliance matrices
  • Orals preparation
  • Color team reviews
POSITIONING
