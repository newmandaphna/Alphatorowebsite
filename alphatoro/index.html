THE THESIS

Three conditions. Zero guesswork about fit.

01

Real commercial book of business.

A firm already delivers at scale in the private sector with documentable past performance, strong margins, and a workforce that shows up.

02

Active SAM registration.

The firm has cleared the federal table stakes. SAM is current, CAGE is issued, representations and certifications are clean.

03

Zero or near-zero federal prime history.

The firm has not yet cracked federal prime work, so the upside is uncapped and the set-aside runway is wide open.

When all three are yes, the firm is a fit.
